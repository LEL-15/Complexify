Instructions



Complexify (verb): To make something more complicated

The goal of this game is to guess the correct complexified version of the given simplified expression.

Each guess must be a valid 6 character math expression that is equal to the simple expression.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to reflect how close the guess is to correct answer. Here are some examples with the prompt \(345x\)

Grey means the tile is not in the expression. Try not to use it again.

Blue means the tile is in the expression but in the wrong place.

Green means the tile is in the correct place in the expression. All green means you win!

Good Luck!

Tips:

  • You are just looking for a complexified version of the simplified prompt. You will not have to solve for x!
  • x is not 0, so \(x/x=1\)
  • Hit the enter tile to submit.

A new Complexify is available each day.
