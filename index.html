Instructions



Complexify the simple algebraic expression in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid 10 character math expression that is equal to the simple expression. Each solutions includes parentheses. Hit the enter tile to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to reflect how close the guess is to the Complexify for which we are looking.

Green means the tile is in the correct place in the expression. All green means you win!

Blue means the tile is in the expression but in the wrong place.

Grey means the tile is not in the expression. Try not to use it again.

Good Luck!

A new Complexify is available each day.
